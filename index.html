The LK Singhania Education Centre at

1987 as a realization of the values and
dreams of the great visionary, Late Lala
Kamlapat Singhania.

LKSEC Story

LKS is a 10+2 co-educational, residential school
affiliated to central board of secondary
education, Delhi. The school inception
on a not-for-profit basis dates
back to the year 1984
with just one room and
with mere 17 students.
It is now situated in a
sprawling campus of 200 acres
and has strength of 1650
students presently.
